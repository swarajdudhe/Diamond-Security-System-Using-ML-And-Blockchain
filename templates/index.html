About Project



Abstract


In the dynamic landscape of technological advancements, Blockchain (BC) has emerged as a transformative force. The diamond industry, poised for evolution as coveted investment assets, faces challenges in provenance, supply chain traceability, and transaction reliability. This paper delves into the adoption of BC in diamonds, exploring its promise in bridging traditional practices with financial markets. In this transformative journey, our paper introduces a visionary fusion of Machine Learning (ML) algorithms—Decision Tree Classifier, Logistic Regression, Random Forest Algorithm, Support Vector Classifier (SVM) and Ensemble Learning Technique. These algorithms discern diamond authenticity and power a recommendation system based on attributes like carat, weight, cut, color, clarity, and symmetry. This seamless ML integration augments our exploration, adding predictive analytics to the broader landscape. Our paper addresses operational issues in the diamond industry and proposes countermeasures. It unveils open research issues and challenges associated with Blockchain integration, including a case study on frameworks like Everledger and Tracr. In essence, this paper explores BC's potential in the diamond industry, unraveling its pros and cons suggesting countermeasures, and shedding light on challenges through case studies. The innovative ML-driven security paradigm we introduce is designed to reshape the industry's future

Technology We Used

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence that enables systems to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. It involves algorithms that learn patterns and make predictions or decisions based on data. By analyzing large datasets, machine learning algorithms can uncover insights and make predictions or decisions with high accuracy. It is widely used in various fields, including image recognition, natural language processing, and recommendation systems.We mostly belive on random forest algorithm for more accuracy

Blockchain

Blockchain is a decentralized, distributed ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers in a tamper-resistant and transparent manner. It enables secure and immutable storage of data without the need for intermediaries, offering transparency, security, and trust in various applications such as cryptocurrency, supply chain management, and voting systems.We mostly belive on Blockchain (BC) for more security
